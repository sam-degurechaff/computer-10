patrick chan

achevements

He is the 2018 Olympic gold medalist in the team event, the 2014 Olympic silver medalist in the men’s and team events, a three-time World Champion 2011–2013, a two-time Grand Prix Final Champion 2010, 2011, a three-time Four Continents Champion 2009, 2012, 2016, and a ten-time Canadian National Champion 2008–2014, 2016–2017. In 2011, Chan was named the recipient of the prestigious Lou Marsh Award as Canada’s top athlete, for setting three world records. Off the ice, he has also received numerous awards and recognitions. In 2007, he won the Chinese Canadian Youth of the Year award, and in 2008 he was named Asian of the Year in arts and sports by Asia Network magazine in 2008.
